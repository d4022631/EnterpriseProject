How many appointments can I make?
There is no limit to the amount of appointments you can have, if there is something you need; Book it!
What is the Booking Block?
The Booking Block is a centralised system that allows businesses and clients to manage bookings, schedules, customer data and online payments. Thus allowing businesses to increase sales, efficiency and allowing their clients to make hassle-free appointments.
I've accidently booked the wrong time, what can I do!
If you have made the booking in the grace period, you will be able to cancel the booking. If not you may have to contact the busisness to correct the booking.
I'm a business and want to set up on Booking Block what do I do?
Follow the sign up instructions and select buisness when choosing your account.
How can I ammend my account details?
Once logged in and you're at your account dashboard, click the 'Manage Account' tab along the top left of the page and ammend any details from, name, address, opening hours etc
Is there an easy way of finding a business I've used in the past?
You will have your account history which will show all of your booking within a certain time frame (6 months?)
